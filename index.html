About me

I am a researcher in Mathematics at Dpto. de Álgebra of Universidad de Sevilla under the supervision of Francisco Castro Jiménez . I earn a Bachelor degree in Mathematics (graduated with Honors), a M.Sc. degree in Mathematics (graduated with Honors) and a M.Ed. degree on Teaching Mathematics, all of them from Universidad de Sevilla.

Research interests

I am interested in algebraic and arithmetic geometry. More precisely, I am interested in applications of $\ell$-adic and rigid cohomology to exponential sums, (arithmetic) $\mathcal{D}$-modules and monodromy of local systems. I am also learning about geometric representation theory, the Langlands program (both geometric and for function fields), Yun's rigidity method and Deligne—Lusztig's theory.

Preprints / Publications

  1. (w/ Antonio Rojas-León) Finite monodromy of some two-parameter families of exponential sums. arXiv:2406.10385
    Abstract. We determine the set of polynomials $f(x)\in k[x]$, where $k$ is a finite field, such that the local system on $\mathbb{G}^2_m$ which parametrizes the family of exponential sums $(s,t)\mapsto \sum_{x\in k}\psi(sf(x)+tx)$ has finite monodromy, in two cases: when $f(x)=x^d+\lambda x^e$ is a binomial and when $f(x)=(x−\alpha)^d(x−\beta)^e$ is of Belyi type.
