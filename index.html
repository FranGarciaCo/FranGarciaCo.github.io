About me

I am a first year PhD student at Depto. de Álgebra of Universidad de Sevilla, under the direction of Francisco Castro Jiménez and Antonio Rojas-León, funded by Ministry of Science of Spain (FPU 23). I earn a Bachelor degree in Mathematics (graduated with Honors), a M.Sc. degree in Mathematics (graduated with Honors) and a M.Ed. degree on Teaching Mathematics, conferred by Universidad de Sevilla.

Research interests

I am interested in algebraic and arithmetic geometry. More precisely, I am interested in applications of $\ell$-adic and rigid cohomology to exponential sums, (arithmetic) $\mathcal{D}$-modules, convolution and monodromy of local systems. I am also learning about geometric representation theory, the Langlands program (both geometric and for function fields), Yun's rigidity method and Deligne—Lusztig's theory.

Preprints / Publications

  1. (w/ Benjamin Church) $\mathrm{SL}_2$-character varieties of $2$-generated groups and failure of weak integrality. arXiv:2410.23233
    Abstract. Let $\ell$ be a prime number, $k$ a positive integer and consider the group $\Gamma_{\ell^k}=\langle a, b\ \vert\ a^{\ell^k(\ell^k-1)}ba^{-\ell^k}b^{-2}\rangle$. We prove that $\Gamma_{\ell^k}$ is not $\mathrm{SL}_2$-weakly integral with obstruction at exactly the prime $\ell$. We also give a general description of the character varieties of $2$-generated groups with a relation of the form $a^{n_1}b^{m_1}a^{n_2}b^{m_2}=1$.
  2. (w/ Antonio Rojas-León) Finite monodromy of some two-parameter families of exponential sums. arXiv:2406.10385
    Abstract. We determine the set of polynomials $f(x)\in k[x]$, where $k$ is a finite field, such that the local system on $\mathbb{G}^2_m$ which parametrizes the family of exponential sums $(s,t)\mapsto \sum_{x\in k}\psi(sf(x)+tx)$ has finite monodromy, in two cases: when $f(x)=x^d+\lambda x^e$ is a binomial and when $f(x)=(x−\alpha)^d(x−\beta)^e$ is of Belyi type.

  • Finite monodromy of some Belyi related two-parameter exponential sums. Luxembourg Number Theory Seminar. Luxembourg; Oct 2024.
    Description. Invited talk at the Luxembourg Number Theory Seminar, where I recalled some well known results about $\ell$-adic local systems (Katz' criterion for finiteness of monodromy and Deligne's equidistribution theorem) and mentioned some new results from the joint paper with Rojas-León.
  • Grupos no fundamentales (Non-fundamental groups). Seminario del Dpto. de Álgebra de la Universidad de Sevilla. Sevilla; Dec 2024.
    Description. Talk at Seminar of Algebra. I recalled the arithmetic obstruction due to Esnault—de Jong for a finitely presented group to be the topological fundamental group of a quasi-projective complex algebraic variety. Then, I reported on my joint work with Church, by introducing a family of groups indexed by prime powers that realizes the failure of SL2-weak integrality at the corresponding prime number. I finished by explaining how the ideas used before allows us to understand the character variety of 2-generated 1-relator (of length 4) groups.
  • Monodromy and equidistribution. RSME's 7th Congress of Young Researchers. Bilbao; Jan 2025.
    Description. Invited talk at RSME's 7th Congress of Young Researchers. I briefly explained how the monodromy of $\ell$-adic local systems governs the distribution of Frobenius traces, and in particular the distribution of exponential sums via Grothendieck's sheaf-to-functions dictionary. Then, I reported on the new families of $\ell$-adic local systems with finite monodromy, found jointly with Rojas-León. I used these pictures to motivate the phenomena of equidistribution.
